# ATAIHQ Landing Page — Master Prompt (Fable 5)

*Paste this entire prompt into Claude Code once Fable 5 is selected.*

---

You're designing a brand-new, awwwards-quality landing page for **ATAIHQ** — a UK AI/digital services company helping SMEs scale through technology. Use Fable 5's full design and animation capability. This is a fresh build — no existing template or draft to reference.

## 1. CORE MESSAGE
- Primary tagline: "We build the technology that grows your business."
- Secondary tagline: "Your AI Department. On Demand."
- Core positioning: ATAIHQ is a one-stop AI/digital department for UK SMEs — helping businesses scale and grow through apps, websites, automation, AI agents, and consultancy.
- Tone: confident, bold, and credible — a mix of approachable and corporate-serious. No AI-sounding fluff, no generic SaaS clichés.

## 2. SCROLL STYLE
- GSAP scroll-triggered animations throughout (smooth fade/slide/scale reveals)
- Three.js abstract animated background — code-generated (particles, gradient mesh, or neural-network-style nodes — your choice), subtle and performant, not distracting
- Aim for an awwwards-level, premium feel — but keep load speed and mobile performance in mind

## 3. COLOUR PALETTE
- Base: #08090d (near-black background), card surfaces #0f1117
- Accent gradient: blue #00AAFF → purple #7B2FFF (matches existing ATAI logo)
- You may introduce a third subtle accent colour (e.g. cyan/teal or warm tone for CTAs) if it strengthens the design — your call
- Fonts available: Exo 2, Share Tech Mono, Syne, DM Sans

## 4. COPY
- Write all real copy — no lorem ipsum or placeholder text
- Short, punchy headlines. Specific claims, not vague promises
- Use UK English and UK-relevant framing throughout
- Stats/numbers can be placeholder for now (clearly realistic-looking, not absurd) — will be swapped for real figures later

## 5. PHOTO + VIDEO
- Generate 2-3 images and 2-3 short looping videos relevant to the services being sold (AI agents, automation, apps, websites, consultancy) — abstract/conceptual, consistent visual style with each other and the brand palette
- Wire these into the relevant sections (hero, service cards, etc.)

## 6. THE SPINE — SECTION STRUCTURE
Build in this rough order (adjust as needed for flow):
1. Hero — tagline, core message, animated background
2. Light stats strip — 3-4 punchy numbers, not text-heavy
3. App Suite (service)
4. Websites (service)
5. Automation (service)
6. AI Agents (service)
7. Consultancy (service)
8. AGI — "Coming Soon" teaser, minimal info, intriguing
9. Deeper facts/content section — more detail, social proof angle
10. Contact — use contact@ataihq.com, info@ataihq.com, support@ataihq.com as appropriate

## ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS
- Fully SEO-friendly: proper semantic HTML, heading hierarchy, meta tags, descriptive alt text on all images
- Fully responsive (mobile + desktop)
- This is a landing page for now — structure it so additional pages can be added later (Websites, AI Agents, Automation, Consultancy as their own full pages down the line)
- Built with HTML/CSS/JS, deployable to Netlify

Use `/gstack-spec` first if you want to plan before building. Take the lead on creative execution within this brief — surprise me.
